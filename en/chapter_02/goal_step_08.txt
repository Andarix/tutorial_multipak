<p>
<h1>Make public stop</h1>
By making the public stop, the player frees himself from maintaining it and also allows sharing the transport network with other players, but he must be careful since by doing this it can no longer be reversed unless he changes to the public service player.
</p>
<p>
<st>Warning: Using this tool is very expensive</st> <em>{prce}</em> for each tile.
</p>
<p>

</p>
